## Summary

As an **authenticated user**, I want **a real activity feed of my recent edits** on `/profile` `ActivityFeedCard` AND a usable "Xem tất cả" expand on FeatureDetail `ActivityRail`, so that **the 4 hardcoded activity rows + the `placeholderToast("Lịch sử hoạt động đầy đủ: tính năng đang phát triển trong v2")` get replaced with real data**.

## Motivation

Two FE surfaces share the same need:

- ProfilePage v4.1 `ActivityFeedCard` (4 hardcoded entries) — surfaced in CR-006 v4.6 audit.
- FeatureDetailPage `ActivityRail` `"Xem tất cả"` button → toast placeholder (existing).

Both want a chronological feed of edits derived from `sections.updated_at` + `updated_by`.

## Proposed scope

**In scope**:

- New endpoint `GET /api/v1/me/activity?limit=20&cursor=<iso>` returning `[{ id, sectionType, featureSlug, projectSlug, featureTitle, updatedAt, verbCode: 'updated'|'created'|'deleted' }]` — chronological desc, cursor pagination via `cursor` = `updated_at` of last row.
- New endpoint `GET /api/v1/projects/:slug/features/:fSlug/activity?limit=20&cursor=` returning feature-scoped feed (used by `ActivityRail "Xem tất cả"`).
- FE: ProfilePage `ActivityFeedCard` reads `useMyActivity()` hook. ActivityRail "Xem tất cả" opens drawer/page reading the feature-scoped endpoint.

**Out of scope**:

- Per-project activity feed (covered by BL-010 ProjectTabs Activity).
- Real-time updates (no WebSocket; cache invalidated on user's own mutation).
- Activity types beyond section edits (no creates/archives/uploads tracked v1 — sections only).
- Activity from comments / reactions (out — no feature).

## Acceptance hint

- **Given** user has 5 recent section edits, **When** GET /me/activity, **Then** 5 entries sorted by `updatedAt` desc with verb "updated".
- **Given** ActivityRail "Xem tất cả" clicked, **When** drawer opens, **Then** feature-scoped paginated feed renders ≤ 20 entries; "Tải thêm" reveals next page.

## Dependencies

- **Blocks**: nothing.
